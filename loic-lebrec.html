Who I Am
Where Data
Meets the Peloton

I'm Loïc Lebrec — a cyclist, data researcher, and cycling journalist based in Paris. I race club-level competitions with WATT Cycling Club, one of the most progressive cycling clubs in France, championing gender diversity in the peloton.

My academic work sits at Paris Dauphine-PSL, where I focus on data analysis and research — from energy forecasting models to health-tech product development. I speak six languages, opening doors to international collaborations with brands, riders, and research teams.

I co-host Radio Fringale, a cycling podcast covering pro racing with depth and passion. And I built Coach Center, a training app for data-driven cyclists.
